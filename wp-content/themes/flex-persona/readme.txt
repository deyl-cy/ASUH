Flex Persona is a dark, minimal WordPress theme designed for those who value elegance and simplicity. Ideal for personal websites, biographies, portfolios, businesses, and digital agencies, its versatile and multipurpose nature makes it a perfect fit for a wide range of creative and professional projects. Flex Persona delivers a sleek aesthetic combined with modern functionality. It's fully compatible with WooCommerce, making it effortless to build stunning online stores or showcase your work with impact.
